GHG Carbon Accounting Onboarding Portal

Welcome! This onboarding process will guide you through the data capture required for accurate, transparent, and auditable GHG reporting. You will enter key corporate, facility, and integration information aligned with GHG Protocol standards. Let's get started!

Purpose & Objectives

  • Establish a consolidated corporate master record
  • Capture detailed sub-records for each facility, subsidiary, or joint venture
  • Integrate energy consumption, emission data, renewable attributes, and offsets
  • Set up ERP and external data integration for continuous updates
  • Ensure full compliance with GHG reporting standards

Example Data & Guidelines

Accurate corporate data is the foundation of your GHG inventory. This initial setup ensures that all subsequent data, from facility-level energy consumption to emission adjustments from renewable purchases, aligns with your organizational structure and reporting standards.
Company Information
"Royal Dutch Shell PLC - UK Operations"

Format: Legal Entity Name – Regional Operation

Regional Operations
"Europe - UK, Netherlands; Middle East - UAE, Saudi Arabia"

Format: Region – Countries (semicolon separated)

Industry Classification
"NAICS: 324110, SIC: 2911"

Format: Classification System: Code

Quick Tips
  • Use official registered company names to ensure compliance accuracy
  • Include all operational regions, even if reporting is limited to specific areas
  • Verify industry codes match your primary business activities
  • Keep documentation ready for any joint ventures or partial ownership entities
