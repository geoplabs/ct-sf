Entity Hierarchy & Sub-Records Creation

Facility & Subsidiary Record Setup

Objective

Create a detailed hierarchical structure by adding sub-records for each facility, subsidiary, or joint venture. This structure should clearly link every operational unit to the corporate master record.

Facility Records

Each facility (e.g. UK-REF-001, US-PR-001) will have its own dedicated data record capturing specific operational details:

  • Unique facility identifiers
  • Operational parameters
  • Energy consumption data
  • Emission factors
Hierarchical Structure

Clear parent-child relationships between corporate entities and operational facilities:

  • Ownership details
  • Boundary specifications
  • Reporting relationships
  • Control approach
Dynamic Organization Tree

Visual representation of your company's operational structure:

  • Interactive hierarchy tree
  • Facility groupings
  • Subsidiary relationships
  • Joint venture links
